Mohammad ﷺ Histroy


Muhammad ﷺ born in 570 CE, was the last prophet of Islam. He received the
first revelation at the age of 40 and preached monotheism, calling people to abandon idol
worship. Despite facing persecution, Muhammad ﷺ and his followers migrated to Medina in 622
CE. There, he established an Islamic state and led military expeditions. In 630 CE, he
peacefully conquered Mecca and forgave his enemies. Muhammad ﷺ's teachings, recorded in the
QURAN and Hadith, promote social justice, women's rights, and religious
tolerance. His life and teachings continue to inspire millions of Muslims worldwide.
